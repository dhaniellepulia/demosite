    About Us

    TEGYOUNG INT’L TRADING, is a trusted distributor of agricultural machineries in the Philippines. We take pride in providing top-tier post-harvest machineries, outstanding after-sales services, and affordable spare parts to our customers. We strive to give our customers high quality machineries maximizing productivity and profits. At TEGYOUNG, we are not just traders; we are partners in progress – where your success is our success..

    Vision

    TEGYOUNG envisions to be the forefront provider of innovative post-harvest machineries. We aim to enhance productivity and cultivate a sustainable future for the Philippines through innovative and high-quality agricultural machineries.

    Mission

    Fueled by the passion for agriculture advancement. TEGYOUNG mission is to supply top-tier post -harvest machineries and contribute to the prosperity of Philippines agriculture.
